the story

Sunday Toffee started the way most good things do — making treats for family and friends during the holidays. After years of people saying "you should sell this," I finally gave it a shot.

It's called Sunday Toffee because that's when you can get it. I make everything myself, one batch at a time, on weekends. It's a one-person operation, about as local as it gets.
